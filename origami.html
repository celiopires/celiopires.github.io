Origami

Prototyping is the best communication tool between designers and developers. For a very long time I wanted a competent tool that would let me explore a wide range of ideas, build rich experiences, polish detailed interactions and at the same time something that people would be able to work with ease. Origami capabilities are extremely powerful, after a couple tutorials followed by some strong black coffees I started to grow a bit of a soft spot for it.

Origami is the perfect tool to clearly understand that there are a means and way around any problem. The preview above is the result of a lot persistence to find the desired outcome. Every page at El Español has a fluid swipe between pages thanks to a lot of experimentation using Origami.
