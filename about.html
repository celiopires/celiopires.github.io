About

I’m Célio. A Portuguese Designer currently based in Madrid, the capital of Spain and working as a product Designer at El Español since 2015 (previously at Observador).

I might have a different opinion from other designers, but I think being a designer is just one of the most interesting jobs out there. Designing human centered products makes all the fun and I’m glad to share my story and to be part of such strong community of designers.

I’m a passionate about Design raised in Portugal and now I’m living and working in Madrid, Spain. My main skills are:

  1. User Experience Design

  2. Product Design

  3. Interaction Design

  4. Design Systems

  5. App Design

  6. Art Direction

  7. Front-end Development

  8. Typography

My workplace has been for a while the newsrooms of the most disruptive digital news products in the countries of Portugal and Spain. It’s incredibly satisfying to see how these projects are gaining followers and reaching the top of the most viewed pages in their countries.
