About me

Bio

I might have a different opinion from other designers, but I think being a designer is one the most interesting jobs out there. Designing human centered products makes all the fun and I’m glad I can share my story and be part of such strong community of professionals.

I’ve born and raised in Portugal. Observador, a Media project based in Lisbon introduced me in 2014 to the Media industry and since then my workplace has been the newsrooms of the two most disruptive Media companies in the countries of Portugal and Spain.

May 19, 2014 - Observador's team posing right after its launch - Lisbon, Portugal

I would never have thought I would be living and working in another country like this, but being involved in projects like El Español, learning a new language, a new culture, meeting the best professionals and some of the most influential persons in the country of Spain, was definitely worth it.

Jun 27, 2015 - First El Español's' public presentation before launch - Madrid, Spain
Some of my coworkers at El Español

Technology and internet are the main responsible for what’s happening in my life, professionally and personally. Being up to date with new tools, new design fields and new ways of thinking makes me feel realised. If I’m not informed about new technologies, it’s like being off the technology world and that’s why I consider I live on the internet, literally.
