About me

I might have a different opinion from other designers, but I think being a designer is one of the most interesting jobs out there.

I began my way through the design world since young age when I got interested in arts and technology. At the age of 15 I designed and coded my first webpage just for fun and then at the age of 21 I used an arduino to launch my first startup.

While I was studying design, Apple released the first iPhone which changed the digital industry forever. Perhaps even Apple couldn’t predict exactly how impactful this release would be but it definitely changed the way we design digital products.

The evolution of Apps like Google Maps, Spotify, Youtube, Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest or Instagram inspired me for years and I’ve never stopped researching how these apps evolved from just an idea to a multi platform app.

Fun fact about me: I may not be very good at developing/coding. But I'm pro at Googling.
