Observador

Role: Product Designer • Dates: Mar 2014 – Apr 2015 • Industry: Media, Communication, Digital news

I set myself on this journey months before Observador’s first release to be in charge of every design step. Identity, user experience and first design versions were settled previously by some Portuguese design agencies, but major permanent decisions were made after my arrival at Observador’s headquarters.

The whole project was carefully thought to be built with the look and feel of a modern online product combining a clean and simple design with the most advanced technology.

Project type

Type: web responsive

Design process

A 12 column layout was used for mobile, tablet and desktop devices. Working with columns for the first time was fun and challenging. Since then, Sketch ↗ has been my most used and favorite design tool.

Technology

Runs on Wordpress and another powerful open source technologies. In less than a year won the award for the “Best news media website” in the country and after a few more awards was mentioned by the Wordpress community as "A case study around the world" for its very impressive architecture.
